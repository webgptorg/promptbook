# Promptbook vs Digital Twins (Personal.ai & Delphi)

Digital Twin platforms like Personal.ai and Delphi are designed to create AI "clones" or "avatars" of real people. They focus on training models on an individual's specific data (emails, chats, videos) to mimic their voice, knowledge, and personality for scaling their time and presence.

While Promptbook also supports the creation of "Digital Twins" through its `PERSONA` and `KNOWLEDGE` commitments, it provides a more open, developer-centric framework. Promptbook allows for more granular control over behavior rules and multi-agent interaction, whereas Personal.ai and Delphi are primarily consumer-facing, hosted products focused on mimicry and monetization.
